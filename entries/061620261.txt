Ive tried to keep diaries in the past, none of them ever get published though. Something about publishing your diaries feels rightly egotistical, it’s kind of like shouting at the world and saying hey look at me, my thoughts are important enough that surely someone would spend their precious time reading them. Im not sure if posting these somewhere is driven by ego or if I like the idea of someone trying to get to know me more. There’s a good chance it’s both. When people make diaries more often than not they will say it’s Private, and just for my eyes. Whenever I’ve made dairies I like the idea of them being private to some extent, but I also when I’m making a diary I like to day dream about someone finding it one day and reading my thoughts. If there was never that possibility I don’t think people would write in diaries. I feel like that 1% chance someone comes across It drives the whole creation process. Why make anything if someone else can’t see it or use it. Frédéric Chopin on his deathbed wanted some of his greatest music to be destroyed, I don’t understand those people who can make things a without eyes on them. Maybe that speaks more to my character than anything. I don’t think I have that selflessness in me. But regardless, this is why I’m going to publish notes somewhere. I haven’t decided yet. I think I just want somewhere to share what I’m really thinking about on a day to day. Sure I have friends and people in my life I could do that with, but with people there’s a dance to follow. You don’t say certain things to some people in your life. You always have to walk around personal boundaries. Which is fine, I wouldn’t want most people in my life talking to me about anything and everything. I don’t know how often or how long I plan on doing this for. But welcome to my project, Sub Rosa.
